Our Story

Founded by Mr. Jignesh Gandhi, Shreeji Stationery has been serving the Ishanpur community with quality stationery products for years. What began as a small neighborhood shop has grown into a trusted destination for all your office, school, and art supply needs.

We take pride in offering a comprehensive selection of products, from basic writing instruments to specialized art materials, all carefully selected to meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Our commitment to quality products, fair pricing, and personalized service has made us the preferred stationery supplier for schools, offices, and artists throughout Ishanpur.
